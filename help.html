Description

The year is 1688, and war has all but destoroyed the land of Kitayama. After a fierce battle, used as a diversion, the enemy kidnapped the empress and fled to the island of

Honshu. The enemy plans to use the empress as leverage and ultimatly win the war. Now, one lone Samurai must travel to Honshu and retrieve the empress.

Armed with his enchanted Katana which belonged to his former master, the lone Samurai must use wit, guile, and stealth to get through Honshu.

The highlighted images presented throughout present various paths for your protagnist to take throughout his journey.

Each path result in different outcomes resulting in a unique experience each time you play. Choose wisely and guide your Samurai to victory!
